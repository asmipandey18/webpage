Early Life

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi into a panjabi Hindu Family.His Father,Prem Nath Kohli,worked as as a criminal lawyer,and his and his Mother,Soraj Kohli,served as a housewife. He has an older borther vikas and an older sister,Bhawna.According to his family,kohli exhibited an early affinity for cricket as a three-year-old.He would pick up a cricket bat and request his father bowl to him.In 1988,the West Delhi Cricket Academy(WDCA)was created.On 30 May of that year,Prem Nath Kohli arranged for his son to meet Rajkumar Sharma.He faced the setback of being unable to secure a place in the under-14 Delhi team,Supposedly due to extraneous factors.Kohli eventually found his way into the under-15 Delhi team.He received training at the academy while simultaneously participating in matches at the Sumeet Dogra academy located at Vasundra Enclave.Here are some key points about Virat kohli's life:

1.Early Interest in Cricket:

  • Virat developed an interest in cricket at a young age,inspired by watching local games.

2.First success:

  • At 14,he was selected for the Delhi under-15 team.

3.National Team Debut:

  • In 2008,he was called up to the indian national team after strong performance in domestic cricket.
